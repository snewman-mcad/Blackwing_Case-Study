Shakers were expert woodworkers who saw beauty in the usefulness of the things they made. Simple shapes, solid joinery, and unadorned materials were the foundation of Shaker design. They build a legacy of beautiful and purposeful work for woodworkers to learn from and follow, demonstrating their commitment to craftsmanship, humble practice, and the use of sustainable materials.
